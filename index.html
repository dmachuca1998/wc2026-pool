Scoring System

Group Stage (72 matches)

+3 Correct Outcome (Win/Draw/Loss) +2 Bonus for Exact Score +0 Wrong

Knockout Stage (32 matches)

+3 R32 Correct Winner +5 R16 Correct +8 QF Correct +12 SF Correct +20 Final Correct +3 Exact KO Score

Champion Progression Bonus

🏆 R32: +10 🏆 R16: +20 🏆 QF: +25 🏆 SF: +38 🏆 Final: +50

If your predicted champion team advances through each knockout round, you earn cumulative bonus points.

How It Works

Before the Tournament:
1. Go to Submit Prediction and enter your name.
2. Predict exact scores for all 72 group stage matches.
3. Predict knockout stage outcomes using group positions (e.g., A1, B2) — no chasing predictions between rounds!
4. Predict scores for each knockout match and select your championship team and total goals estimate.
5. Submit once — predictions are final and private. Only the organizer can view them.

During the Tournament:
6. As real matches are played, the organizer enters actual scores in the Admin Panel.
7. The system automatically compares every participant's predictions against real results:
  • Group Stage: Correct outcome = 3 pts, plus 2 bonus for exact score (total 5 for exact).
  • Knockout: Points increase by round (3→5→8→12→20). Exact KO score = +3 bonus.
  • Champion Progression: R32: +10, R16: +20, QF: +25, SF: +38, Final: +50.
  • Bonus: Closest total goals (+30).
8. The Leaderboard updates automatically!
