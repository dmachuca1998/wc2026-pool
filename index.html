Scoring System

Group Stage (72 matches)

+3 Correct Outcome (Win/Draw/Loss) +2 Bonus for Exact Score +0 Wrong

Knockout Stage (32 matches)

+5 R32 Correct Winner +7 R16 Correct +9 QF Correct +11 SF Correct +14 Final Correct +3 Exact KO Score

Champion Progression Bonus

🏆 R32: +10 🏆 R16: +12 🏆 QF: +15 🏆 SF: +18 🏆 Final: +25 🏆 Champion Winner: +35

Cumulative: if your champion team reaches each round AND wins the final, you earn up to 115 total bonus points.

Other Bonuses

🥉 +2 per Correct 3rd-Place Qualifier ⚽ +15 Closest Total Goals

3rd-Place Qualifiers: You pick 8 of the 12 groups whose 3rd-place team you think will advance to the knockout round. Earn +2 for each group you correctly identify.

Note: Penalty shootout goals are excluded from the total goals count. Only goals scored during regular time and extra time count.

Knockout Update Window: After the group stage ends, the organizer will open a window for you to revise your knockout predictions based on final group standings.

How It Works

Before the Tournament:
1. Go to Submit Prediction and enter your name.
2. Predict exact scores for all 72 group stage matches.
3. Predict knockout stage outcomes using group positions (e.g., A1, B2).
4. Predict scores for each knockout match and select your championship team and total goals estimate.
5. Submit once — predictions are final and private. Use View My Predictions on the Submit tab to review your picks from any device using your name and password.

After Group Stage (Knockout Update Window):
6. After Match 72 (June 27th), the organizer opens a Knockout Update Window.
7. Participants can update only their knockout predictions (3rd-place picks, match winners, scores, champion, goals) — group stage predictions remain locked.
8. The window closes before Match 73 (Round of 32) begins.

During the Knockout Stage:
9. As real matches are played, the organizer enters actual scores in the Admin Panel.
10. The system automatically compares every participant's predictions against real results:
  • Group Stage: Correct outcome = 3 pts, plus 2 bonus for exact score (total 5 for exact).
  • Knockout: Points increase by round (3→5→8→12→20). Exact KO score = +3 bonus.
  • Champion Progression: R32: +10, R16: +12, QF: +15, SF: +18, Final: +25, Winner: +35.
  • Bonus: Closest total goals (excluding knockout round Penalty Shootouts) (+5).
11. The Leaderboard updates automatically!

Group Stage Predictions

Predict the exact score for each of the 72 group stage matches.

How group stage scoring works:
• +3 points for predicting the correct outcome (Win, Draw, or Loss) — even if the score is wrong
• +2 bonus points for predicting the exact final score (total 5 points for an exact match)
• 0 points if you predict the wrong outcome

Important: Group stage matches CAN end in a draw — there are no penalty shootouts in the group stage. If you predict a draw and the match ends in a draw, you earn the outcome points regardless of the exact score.

Step 2: Predict Each Knockout Match

How to predict each knockout match:

Each match shows two teams identified by their group position (e.g., "A2" = 2nd place from Group A, "3rd*" = a third-place qualifier).

1. Pick the winner: Click on the team you think will advance. Your selection highlights in gold. This is the team you predict will win the match — whether they win in regular time, extra time, or penalties.

2. Enter the score: Type the predicted final score (e.g., 2 - 1). This is the score at the end of regular/extra time. If you think the match goes to penalties, enter the score that led to penalties (e.g., 1 - 1) and still click the team you think will win the shootout.

Example: You think A1 (Mexico) beats 3rd* in a penalty shootout after a 1-1 draw → Click "A1" as winner, enter score 1 - 1.
Example: You think C1 (Brazil) beats F2 in regular time 3-0 → Click "C1" as winner, enter score 3 - 0.

The winner pick (+3 to +20 pts by round) and exact score (+3 pts) are scored independently. You earn points for each one you get right.
